Koala

Koalas come from Australia. They are tiny marsupials. It's closes relatives the animal kingdom are wombats. They can live 13 to 18 years in the wild. You may recognize the koala from baby changing stations across the country because apparently they're associated with that. Go figure. Our zoo has lots of koalas. Koalas are a vulnerable species, so we need to lookout for them.
